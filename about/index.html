About Me

Hi! I’m Magnus, this is my website, where I will hopefully write about things I find interesting. I am third year PhD student at the University of Manchester, working with Mauricio Alvarez on integrating physical information into Gaussian process models. I am interested in many different areas of machine learning, but in particular I enjoy those that have some connection to physics (fortunately there are many!).

Outside of work I like to go running, play music, and look after my plants.
