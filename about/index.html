Magnus Ross

Hi! I’m Magnus, this is my website, hopefully you will find something interesting here. I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow based in the AI Center at UCL. I’m interested in forecasting, uncertainty quantification, and probabilitic ML in general. I am also working on using ML to help combat antimicrobial resistance, as part of the Digital Health Hub for AMR.

Previously, I did my PhD at the University of Manchester working with Mauricio Alvarez on integrating physical information into Gaussian process models. You can find my thesis here. For a time I worked for the dark side (a trading firm), building forecasting models for trading power and other energy commodities.

Outside of work I like to go running, play music, and sit in the sauna.
