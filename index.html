About Me

Hi! I'm Magnus, this is my website, where I will hopefully write about things I find interesting. I have just finished doing a Master's degree in physics at the University of Bristol, and this autumn I will be joining the machine learning group at the University of Sheffield to do a PhD. I am interested in many different areas of machine learning, but in particular I enjoy those that have some connection to physics (fortunately there are many!). In the past I have worked on projects applying deep learning to more effciently analyse data from two experiments searching for new fundemental physics: CMS at the LHC, and LUX-ZEPLIN.

Outside of work I like to go running, play music, and tell my friends about things I think would make funny tweets.

You can find a formal CV here, and follow me on Github here if you like that sort of thing!
